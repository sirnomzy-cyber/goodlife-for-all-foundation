FAQ

Common Questions

Our programs are open to community members who meet each program's specific eligibility criteria, generally based on need, age, or location. Selection is transparent and community-informed.

Reach out through our Partners page or Contact form. Our team will schedule a discussion to understand your goals and design a partnership that fits.

Each program uses a documented selection process involving community leaders, needs assessments and clear eligibility criteria, reviewed periodically for fairness.

84% of every donation goes directly to program delivery. The remainder covers essential monitoring, evaluation and administrative costs, all disclosed in our annual report.

Visit our Get Involved page to see current volunteer opportunities and submit an application. Our team will follow up with next steps.
