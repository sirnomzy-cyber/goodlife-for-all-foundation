What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, understand how visitors use the site, and improve overall performance.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your display preferences, such as dark mode or accessibility settings.
  • Understand how visitors navigate and use our website, so we can improve it.
  • Support the secure and reliable delivery of our forms and donation tools.

Types Of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies — required for the website to function properly, such as remembering your cookie consent choice.
  • Preference Cookies — remember choices you make, like dark mode or text size, so you don't have to reset them on every visit.
  • Analytics Cookies — help us understand which pages are most useful to visitors, so we can improve our content and programmes.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services embedded in our website, such as font or icon providers. We do not control these cookies directly; please refer to the relevant third party's own cookie or privacy policy for details.

Managing Cookies

You can accept or manage cookies through the cookie banner shown when you first visit our site. Most web browsers also allow you to control cookies through their settings, including blocking or deleting them. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect how parts of this website function.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the cookies we use or for legal reasons. Please check back periodically for updates.
