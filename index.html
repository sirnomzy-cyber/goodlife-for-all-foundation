What We Do

Ten Programmes, One Shared Purpose

Flagship

Safe Motherhood Initiative (SMI)

Maternal health education, birth preparedness and safe delivery support.

Youth Empowerment & Skills Academy

Digital, vocational and entrepreneurial skills training.

Women Empowerment Initiative

Financial literacy and business support.

Community Health & Wellness Initiative

Medical outreach and preventive healthcare.

Education & Future Leaders Initiative

Scholarships and school support.

Community Food Security Programme

Food assistance and nutrition education for vulnerable households.

Civic Education & Leadership Initiative

Civic responsibility and democratic participation.

Community Safety & Peacebuilding Programme

Peacebuilding and public safety awareness.

Senior Citizens Support Programme

Welfare support and social engagement for older adults.

Clean Communities & Environmental Action Initiative

Sanitation, tree planting and climate action.
