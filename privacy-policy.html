Introduction

The GoodLife For All Foundation ("GLAF", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of everyone who visits our website, applies to volunteer, donates, or otherwise interacts with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using this website, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly — such as your name, email address, phone number, and message content when you fill out our contact, donation, volunteer, or partnership forms.
  • Donation-related information — including donation amount, currency, and, where applicable, a screenshot or proof of a bank transfer submitted for documentation purposes.
  • Automatically collected information — such as browser type, device type, and general usage data collected through cookies and similar technologies.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiries, applications, and messages.
  • Process and acknowledge donations, and match bank transfers to the correct donor.
  • Keep you updated on our programmes, impact, and opportunities to get involved, where you have opted in.
  • Improve our website and the experience of visitors.
  • Meet legal, accounting, and reporting obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Donations & Payment Information

When you donate by bank transfer, we ask for your name, email address, and a screenshot of your completed transaction for documentation and reconciliation purposes only. We do not store full bank account or card details on our servers. Where online payment gateways (such as Paystack) are used, your payment information is processed directly and securely by that provider under its own privacy policy.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience and understand how the site is used. For full details on what cookies we use and how to manage them, please see our Cookie Policy.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to help operate this website, including:

  • Web3Forms — to process and deliver form submissions (contact, donation, volunteer/partnership applications, and newsletter sign-ups) to our email inbox.
  • Google Fonts & Font Awesome — to load fonts and icons used across the site.
  • WhatsApp — if you choose to contact us via our WhatsApp chat link.

These providers may process limited technical data (such as IP address) as part of delivering their service. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose it was collected for — such as responding to your enquiry, processing your donation, or maintaining accurate financial and programme records — after which it is securely deleted or anonymized, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Your Rights

In line with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and general data protection best practice, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to our legal and record-keeping obligations.
  • Withdraw consent to marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us at goodlifeforallfoundation@gmail.com or through our Contact page.
